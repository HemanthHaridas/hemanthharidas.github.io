Bio
I hail from Kerala, a southern state in India. After completeing my schooling in Kerala, I moved to Pondicherry Central University to pursue a masters in Chemistry. I obtained my M.Sc. (Integrated) in Chemistry from Pondicherry Central University in 2018. I then moved to Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar, to pursue my Ph.D. in computational chemistry. I work with Dr. Sairam S. Mallajosyula at IITGn, where we focus on development of FF parameters that conform to CHARMM parameterization stratergy.
